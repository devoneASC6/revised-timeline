The timeline of Naruto Uzumaki(Part 1)

The birth of Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki was born to Minato Namikaze and Kushina Uzumaki on October 10th during the Nine-Tails attack on the Hidden Leaf Village.

Graduation from the Academy

After proving himself in a battle against an evil ninja in which he finds out he is the jinchuriki of the Nine-Tails, Naruto graduates from the Ninja Academy. He is later assigned to team 7 under Kakashi Hatake alongside Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno.

Fight against the One-Tails

During the 3rd stage of the Chunin Exams, the Leaf is invaded by the Hidden Sand and the Hidden Sound. Naruto fights against the One-Tails jinchuriki, Gaara, and wins also changing Gaara's mindset in the process.

Naruto vs. Sasuke

After Sasuke betrays the village in serch of power for revenge, Naruto and his friends chase Sasuke and his group down. This eventually leads to a heated fight between Naruto and Sasuke at the Final Valley which ends in Naruto's loss.

Naruto's training journey

Naruto leaves the village for 3 years with his teacher, Jiraiya, to train whith him and be prepared for the next time he faces Sasuke and can bring him back.
